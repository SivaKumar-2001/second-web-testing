Our Story

Founded in 1998, Compass Housing Society began with a simple mission: to help people find not just houses, but homes where memories are made. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted real estate agencies in the region.

Our founder, Michael Anderson, believed that real estate should be about more than just transactions. It should be about understanding people's dreams, their aspirations, and their stories.

Our Mission

To guide individuals and families in finding their perfect homes by providing exceptional service, expert advice, and innovative solutions that make the real estate journey seamless and rewarding.

We are committed to building lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and a deep understanding of our clients' unique needs and aspirations.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected real estate partner in the region, known for transforming the way people experience property buying and selling through innovation, excellence, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

We envision communities where every family finds not just a house, but a true home that becomes the foundation for their happiest memories.
